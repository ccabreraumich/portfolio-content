Are you EV Ready?

"ReadyEV is a site designed to explain the benefits of driving an electric vehicle (EV) and to convince you that driving an EV is not as far out of your reach as you might think! This site will address commonly asked questions about how to transition from driving a traditional gas-powered vehicle to driving an electric one!

EVs have become so popular that you're sure to find one that fits your lifestyle. This site will provide answers to four different categories of common quesitons from car drivers, related to types of vehicles available, charging options, range of the vehicle, and vehicle maintenance.
