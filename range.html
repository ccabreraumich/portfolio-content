How far can I drive?

In the future, there will be charging stations at every location where we traditionally have gasoline-based fuel. While we are not there yet, we are moving in that directly very quickly.

The amount of charging stations has increased enormously over the past 10 years.


Below is a map from the Department of Energy showing how prevalant charging stations have become throught the United States. You are sure to find a charging station within reach on your next road trip!

There are smartphone apps available, such as Plugshare, to help you plan your next trip and locate charging stations along your route.
